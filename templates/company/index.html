PROJECT
ENVISION

a venture by Enactus VIT, is targeted at the upliftment of visually challenged by training and employing them at a Business Process Outsourcing unit.

Features

The employees at Envision wiil help you sell your products or special plans to people by making calls on a daily basis.

With the help of Envision, you and your company can publicize and promote any future planned events by inviting people through calls

Testing a product or plan among the common mass by having Envision employees ask people as to whether or not they would be interested in trying out the new idea.

Promote new offers on products or plans by reaching out to your customers via telephone marketing and spread the word.

Monitor in Real-time

The technical team at Enactus VIT, Vellore has designed an app that would allow the employees to make calls to people, and also provide inputs on how the call went.
Through real-time monitoring one is able to check the performance and analyze the reports which makes it easier to generate a feedback and hence work, adapt and improve accordingly.

Why Us?

Project Envision, a venture by Enactus in association with CSRD-RS VIT University, centred at Vellore, has a motive of training and employing the visually challenged in a fully functional call centre. The aim of this project is to ensure that barriers are broken down in the community by proving that there is no significant gap in the employability skills of visually handicapped people and everyone else in many fields. Currently, there are multiple projects by various NGOs that focus on helping physically handicapped people but there is a major scarcity of initiatives for visually handicapped.
